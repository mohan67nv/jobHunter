## Constraints & Guidelines

### Must Follow:
1. **No fabrication** - CV remains 100% truthful
2. **Quantifiable metrics required** - Every bullet needs numbers (%, time, count, scale)
3. **Natural integration** - Keywords must fit contextually, not feel "stuffed"
4. **Lean optimization** - Modify only 30-40% of total bullets (quality over quantity!)
5. **Preserve achievements** - Don't remove original accomplishments to fit keywords
6. **Word count discipline:**
   - Total CV: 700-900 words (US/UK market standard for 5-10 years experience)
   - Per bullet: 60-90 words maximum
   - Skills section: 80-100 words total

### Keyword Integration Rules:
- **Action verbs:** Use strong verbs at bullet start (Architected, Developed, Optimized, Led, Implemented)
- **Technical nouns:** Integrate naturally in technical descriptions
- **Tools/frameworks:** Mention with context (e.g., "using PyTorch and TensorFlow" not just "PyTorch, TensorFlow")
- **Avoid keyword stuffing:** No lists of technologies without context

### ATS Optimization Targets:
- **Keyword density:** 10-15 JD keywords per page (for technical roles)
- **First-page keywords:** 60% of critical JD keywords must appear on page 1
- **Skills section:** Must contain all JD-mentioned tools/frameworks
